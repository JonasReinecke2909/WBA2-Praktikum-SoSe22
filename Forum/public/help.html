First Step: Create an account (if you haven't already). Just click on the Menu an select "Sign up". You will have to enter your First Name, Last Name, E-Mail, Username and a Password. Dont forget to pic a secure Password (hello is not a secure password...)!

Second Step: Your Account is now created and you are ready to sign in. Switch to the Menu once again an click "Sign in". There you need to enter your E-Mail or Username and your Password.

Third Step: Now you are ready to go! If you have a question about a topic in the IT-Security sector just open the Menu up and klick "Ask Question". There you can give your question a quick heading and explain it more accurate in the box below.

Important! Make sure to choose the right categorie for your question. Then just klick submit and you are done. How amazing! Your just asked your first question on the ITS-Forum page. And answering is even easier! Just open one of the question from the front page or the different categories and the answer area will be just right there.

You've just learned how to ask and answer Questions! Have fun!
